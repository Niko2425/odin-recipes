Instant Ramen Recipe!

Description:

Basically guys this is what you make when you're lazy and don't wanna cook

Ingredients Needed:

  • Cup-A-Noodles
  • Boiled Water

Steps to make:

  1. Open the ramen
  2. Boil water and pour it into the ramen
  3. Wait about 15-20 minutes
  4. Eat up and most of all, ENJOY!
